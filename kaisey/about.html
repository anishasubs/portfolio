What is Kaisey?

Kaisey is an intelligent scheduling assistant designed for MBA students. It connects to your Google Calendar to help you optimize your day by balancing academics, recruiting, networking, and wellness.

Features

  • AI-powered schedule optimization around your existing calendar events
  • Brain Dump Planner — dump your tasks and get an organized schedule
  • Smart recommendations based on your priorities and preferences
  • Google Calendar integration for real-time event syncing

How it uses your data

Kaisey accesses your Google Calendar to read and manage events. All data is processed in your browser — nothing is stored on external servers. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.
