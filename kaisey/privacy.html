Kaisey Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

What Kaisey Does

Kaisey is an MBA scheduling co-pilot that connects to your Google Calendar to help you optimize your day.

Data We Access

When you sign in with Google, Kaisey requests access to:

  • Your Google Calendar events (read and write)
  • Your basic profile info (name, email, profile picture)

How We Use Your Data

Your calendar data is used solely to display and manage your schedule within the app. Your data is processed entirely in your browser and is not sent to or stored on any external server.

Data Storage

Kaisey stores your Google access token in your browser's local storage. No personal data is stored on any server. You can clear this at any time by clearing your browser data or logging out.

Third-Party Services

If you provide an OpenAI API key, your schedule data may be sent to OpenAI's API for AI-powered scheduling suggestions. This is optional and only occurs if you explicitly add an API key.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third parties.
